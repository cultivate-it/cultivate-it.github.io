Why Cultivate?

A strength is not what you are good at, it is what you enjoy! We need to get better in organisations at matching people to roles to enable top performance

Who we are

We are two people passionate about getting the right people into the right roles through data driven selection and feedback.

What we do

We use a number of external data sources and combine this with enterprise data to have internal skills transparency and the full picture on talent.

Our philosophy

Our philosophy is that life is too short to be performing the wrong role and enterprises need to up their performance through the development of their staff and their skills.
